Privacy Policy for Convo

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

Convo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Convo.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you use the application:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and profile picture provided during registration or Google Sign-In.
  • User Content: Messages, photos, and posts that you create and share within the app’s chat and feed features.
  • Technical Data: Device information such as model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID) for performance monitoring and advertising.

2. Permissions We Request

To provide a full experience, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos for profile updates and sharing in chats/posts.
  • Storage/Media: To access and upload existing images from your device gallery.
  • Internet & Network State: To sync data with our servers and enable real-time communication.
  • Bluetooth: To support proximity-based features and connectivity.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate user-to-user communication (Chat and Social Feed).
  • To personalize your user profile and settings.
  • To monitor and analyze app usage to improve user experience.
  • To display relevant advertisements through third-party partners.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate several third-party services to power Convo. These services may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services & Firebase: Used for authentication, cloud database management, and push notifications.
  • ImgBB: A third-party image hosting service used to store images uploaded by users.
  • AdMob (Google) & Start.io: Advertising platforms that use device identifiers to serve personalized ads.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. We use industry-standard security protocols, including Firebase Security Rules and in-app Privacy PIN features, to protect your information. However, please be aware that no electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data and account at any time through the app settings or by contacting us directly.

7. Children’s Privacy

Convo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our servers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
